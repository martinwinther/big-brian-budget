summary: How to buy decent extra-virgin olive oil (EVOO) in DK without overspending.

---

Good EVOO doesn't have to be expensive. Look for harvest date, dark bottles, and try smaller 500 ml formats to keep it fresh. For cooking, you can use modest-heat with EVOO and finish cold for flavor.
